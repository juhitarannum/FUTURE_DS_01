# Task 1 - Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard developed in Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance by analyzing revenue, profit, orders, product performance, regional sales, and category-wise sales distribution. The objective is to help stakeholders monitor key business metrics and identify opportunities for growth and improvement.

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers and Filters
* KPI Cards
* Data Cleaning and Formatting

## Dataset

The dataset contains business sales transaction records, including:

* Order Date
* Product Name
* Category
* Region
* Revenue
* Profit
* Quantity Sold
* Order Information

The data was cleaned, organized, and analyzed to create meaningful visualizations and business insights.

## KPIs

The dashboard tracks the following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

1. Total Revenue: $13.89M
2. Total Profit: $2.79M
3. Total Orders: 5,500
4. Average Order Value: $2,526.28
5. Profit Margin: 20.05%

## Analysis Performed

* Revenue trend analysis over time.
* Top-selling products analysis.
* Regional sales performance comparison.
* Category-wise sales distribution analysis.
* Profitability assessment using profit margin.
* Order volume and average order value evaluation.

## Dashboard Features

* Interactive KPI cards.
* Revenue trend visualization.
* Top 6 selling products chart.
* Regional performance analysis.
* Sales by category pie chart.
* Dynamic filtering using slicers.
* Clean and user-friendly dashboard layout.

## Key Insights

* The business generated a total revenue of $13.89M with a profit of $2.79M.
* The overall profit margin remained healthy at 20.05%.
* Microwave emerged as the highest-selling product.
* The Central region recorded the highest sales performance.
* Sales are fairly distributed across product categories, indicating a balanced product portfolio.
* Revenue remained consistent throughout the analyzed period.

## Recommendations

* Increase inventory and promotional efforts for top-performing products.
* Focus on improving sales in lower-performing regions.
* Analyze customer demand patterns to optimize product offerings.
* Continue monitoring profit margins to maintain profitability.
* Implement targeted marketing campaigns for underperforming categories.
* Leverage successful strategies from the Central region in other regions.
